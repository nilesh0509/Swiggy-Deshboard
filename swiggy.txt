Problem Statement:

    BUsiness Requirement Development (BRD) : KPI's
        1.Total Sales :- Overall Revanue Generated from food orders
        2.Average Rating :- Customer Satisfaction level across all restaurants.
        3.Average Order Value :- Revanue Per Order
        4.Rating Count :- Total number of Customer reviews.
        5.Total orders :- Number of food Order received.

    BUsiness Requirement Development (BRD) : Chart's
        1.Monthly Sales Trend :- Shows how total sales fluctuate Month by Month.
        2.Daily Sales Trend :- Highlights order and revanue variations across days of the week.
        3.Total Sales by Food Type(Veg vs non-veg) :- Compares revanue contribution by cuisine type.
        4.Total Sales by state(Map Visualization) :- Display state-wise revanue distribution.
        5.Quarterly Performance Summary :- Combines Sales, Ratings, Orders by Quarter.
        6.Top 5 Cities by Sales :- Identify Landing Cities contributing the most revanu.
        7.Weekly Trend Analysis :- Monitors Weekly fluctuations in sales to identify consistency or peak period.
